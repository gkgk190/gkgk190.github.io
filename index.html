our story

From one small piece of clay to a whole creative space.

  • 2022.03

    First Touch of Clay

    We first discovered the charm of pottery by simply touching clay. Sitting at the wheel brought a surprising sense of calm.

  • 2022.09

    Starting to Learn

    We explored various classes and started to understand the joy and depth of making pottery.

  • 2023.05

    Sharing with Friends

    Creating, sharing, and even displaying our works with friends brought us simple but lasting happiness.

  • 2024.02

    Building Our Own Studio

    We finally built our own space and started classes. A place where anyone can relax, create, and feel at home.
